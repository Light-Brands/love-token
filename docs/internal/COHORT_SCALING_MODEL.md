# PROJECT OVERVIEW

## Cohort-Based Scaling Model with Mission Funding

*(Internal / Partner-Facing Version)*

---

## 1. What We Are Building

We are building a mission-aligned digital currency ecosystem designed for long-term growth, cultural integrity, and real-world impact.

The system is intentionally structured so that:

- Early participants take more responsibility and longer lockups
- Participation is capped and phased, not unlimited
- Supply is locked early, reducing speculative pressure
- Growth happens in controlled waves
- The nonprofit mission (TWGE) is funded structurally as the ecosystem grows

**This is not a typical open-market token launch.**
**It is a stewardship-first participation model.**

---

## 2. Structural Separation (Foundational)

There are two aligned but independent components:

### A. The World's Greatest Experiment (TWGE) — Nonprofit

- Community membership
- Meditation, participation, contribution
- Love Farms and real-world projects
- Receives membership fees and protocol contributions
- **Does not sell or manage the currency**

### B. Digital Currency Protocol (Separate Entity)

- Issues and manages the currency
- Defines participation cohorts and caps
- Enforces staking and lockups
- Routes protocol fees to TWGE

**This separation ensures:**

- Regulatory clarity
- Mission integrity
- Long-term trust

---

## 3. Membership as the Gate (Values Filter)

To participate in the digital currency ecosystem:

- Individuals or organizations must first become TWGE members
- Membership reflects alignment, contribution, and participation
- Membership is not framed as purchasing financial access
- Membership does not guarantee allocation or outcomes

**Membership ensures:**

- Everyone contributes something real
- Culture is established before scale
- The ecosystem remains mission-led, not speculative

## 7. Ongoing Protocol Revenue (Beyond Membership)

In addition to membership funding, the protocol generates ongoing revenue:

### Transaction Fees

| Fee Type | Rate |
|----------|------|
| From sender | 2.2% |
| From receiver | 2.2% |
| **Total per transaction** | **4.4%** |

### Fee Allocation

| Destination | Percentage |
|-------------|------------|
| Protocol treasury / ecosystem support | 50% |
| TWGE (nonprofit) | 50% |

**This means:**

- As usage increases, TWGE funding increases
- Impact scales with activity, not speculation
- Love Farms expand as the network grows

## 8. Why This Model Is Powerful Long-Term

From a systems perspective, this model creates:

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| Early supply lock-up | Reduced sell pressure |
| Capped participation | Whale protection |
| Phased access | Controlled growth |
| Repeatable cohorts | Scalable expansion |
| Embedded mission funding | Real-world impact |
| Strong culture | Established before mass adoption |

**As the currency becomes more valuable:**

- New cohorts can open at market value
- Participation remains capped and values-gated
- Earlier participants are not diluted unfairly
- TWGE funding increases with scale

---

## 9. How Value Can Emerge (Without Promises)

The system does not promise appreciation.

However, it is intentionally designed so that:

- Supply is constrained early
- Holding periods are long
- Demand can grow while liquidity remains limited
- Trust, belief, and participation compound

**Any increase in value would be:**

- Emergent
- Market-driven
- Based on adoption and alignment
- **Not guaranteed or marketed**
