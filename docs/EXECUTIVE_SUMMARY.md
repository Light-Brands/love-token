# Love Token - Executive Summary

**Love Token** is an initiative by The World's Greatest Experiment (TWGE) to create the world's first "Love Economy" — a system where **meditation generates real economic value** for underserved communities.

---

## Core Concept

Community members ("Love Farmers") earn digital currency by meditating. They spend these credits at local vendors, who redeem them for local fiat currency backed by a donor-funded liquidity pool. Credits are burned upon redemption to maintain stability.

---

## Dual-Currency System

- **Love Points (LP)** — Global, non-monetary recognition tokens anyone can earn by meditating (min 2:22 session)
- **Love Credits (LVC)** — Local economic currency for verified Love Farmers only, earned at 0.01 LVC/minute, spendable at partner vendors

---

## Key Design Principles

- **Dignity over charity** — participants earn, not receive
- **Local circulation** — credits stay within communities, not extracted
- **Non-speculative** — credits are non-transferable and burned on redemption
- **Accessible** — symbol-based UI for low-literacy users

---

## Additional Features

- **Energy Integration Layer** — collective meditation milestones unlock real infrastructure (lighting, water, refrigeration)
- **Lumi** — an AI guide for user onboarding
- **Donor model** — monthly subscriptions ($50/$100/custom) with daily impact updates
